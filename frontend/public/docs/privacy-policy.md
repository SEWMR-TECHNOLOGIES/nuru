# NURU WORKSPACE - PRIVACY POLICY

**Effective Date: February 2025**

Nuru Workspace ("Nuru", "we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the Nuru platform.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

We collect information you provide when creating and using your account:

- Full name and display name
- Phone number
- Email address
- Profile photo and bio
- Payment and billing details
- Event information (dates, locations, guest lists, budgets)
- Service listings and portfolio content (images, videos, descriptions)
- Messages sent through the platform
- Posts, moments, and comments
- Contribution and ticket purchase details
- Support requests and feedback

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the platform, we may automatically collect:

- Device information (type, operating system, browser)
- IP address and approximate location
- Usage data (pages visited, features used, time spent)
- Session information and cookies
- Referral source

### 1.3 Media Content

We collect and store media that you upload, including:

- Profile photos and avatar images
- Event cover images and gallery photos
- Vendor portfolio images and videos
- Intro videos and audio recordings
- Moment photos and videos
- Post images and attachments
- Photo Library images shared by Vendors
- Invitation card designs

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Create and manage your account
- Process bookings, payments, and contributions
- Facilitate connections between Organisers and Vendors
- Send event updates, reminders, and booking notifications
- Process and verify ticket purchases
- Enable social features (posts, moments, circles, communities)
- Deliver and display Photo Libraries
- Enable messaging between Users
- Process NFC Card issuance and management
- Resolve disputes between Users
- Improve platform security and prevent fraud
- Analyse usage patterns to improve our services
- Send important service announcements
- Comply with legal obligations

---

## 3. Media and Content Processing

### 3.1 Image and Video Processing

- Uploaded images and videos may be compressed, resized, or optimized for platform performance.
- Thumbnails and previews may be generated automatically from uploaded media.
- We do not alter the substantive content of uploaded media.

### 3.2 Content Visibility

- Content visibility depends on the privacy settings chosen by the User.
- Public content may be visible to all platform Users.
- Event-specific content is visible to event participants based on their roles.
- Photo Library content visibility is controlled by the Vendor's chosen privacy settings.
- Posts and moments are visible based on the User's sharing preferences.

### 3.3 Content Retention After Deletion

- When you delete content, we will remove it from public visibility promptly.
- Cached or backed-up copies may persist for a reasonable period.
- Content shared with or downloaded by other Users cannot be recalled after sharing.
- Event-related content may be retained for dispute resolution purposes.

---

## 4. Payment Data

- Payment information is processed through third-party payment providers.
- Nuru does not store full card details or sensitive financial information on its servers.
- Transaction records (amounts, dates, parties involved) are retained for accounting and dispute resolution.

---

## 5. Data Sharing

We may share your data with:

- **Payment providers** to process transactions
- **Event participants** as necessary for event coordination (e.g., guest lists, RSVP status)
- **Vendors and Organisers** to facilitate bookings and service delivery
- **Law enforcement** when required by law or court order
- **Regulatory authorities** when required by applicable regulations

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

We do not share your data with advertisers for targeted advertising purposes.

---

## 6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data, including:

- SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit
- Encrypted data storage with restricted access
- Regular security reviews and updates
- Secure authentication mechanisms
- Access controls limiting employee access to personal data

However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

---

## 7. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as necessary for:

- Providing our services to you
- Legal compliance and regulatory requirements
- Dispute resolution and fraud prevention
- Financial record-keeping

When data is no longer needed, it will be securely deleted or anonymized.

---

## 8. Cookies and Tracking

- We use cookies and similar technologies to maintain sessions and improve user experience.
- We may use analytics tools to understand platform usage patterns.
- You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

---

## 9. Children's Privacy

- Nuru is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age.
- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
- If we become aware that we have collected data from a child, we will delete it promptly.

---

## 10. User Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data held by us
- **Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your personal data and account
- **Portability**: Request your data in a portable, machine-readable format
- **Objection**: Object to certain processing of your data
- **Restriction**: Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@nuru.tz.

Some data may be retained even after deletion requests where required by law or for legitimate business purposes (such as dispute resolution or fraud prevention).

The platform may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

## 12. International Data

Your data may be processed and stored in locations outside your country of residence. By using the platform, you consent to such transfer and processing, subject to appropriate safeguards.

- Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
